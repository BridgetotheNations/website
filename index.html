About us

Bridge to the Nations was born with the vision to become a bridge to all the nations, facilitating the way to spread the gospel in all the Earth.

Our mission is cooperate with the edification of the saints in UK for the work of the ministry and preparation for the last days. Many people are living their life's journey without purpose, living in a cyclical way, seeming to be always the same thing. Our heart's desire is that the nations may understand what God's vision is for their life’s and help them see that they are part of God's plan.
